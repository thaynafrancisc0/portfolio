Apollo

Apollo is a project geared towards optimizing the day-to-day activities of our internal stakeholders. My primary objective within this initiative is to refine user flow, ensure consistency in design standards, and elevate overall usability. With a keen focus on design methodologies, I collaborate closely with the team to implement effective front-end development strategies, ensuring a seamless and intuitive user experience.

Honco

Honco is an application designed to provide invaluable support to individuals navigating the complexities of cancer treatment. My involvement in this project encompasses various stages, from initial discovery and ideation to prototyping and front-end development. By leveraging user-centric design principles and empathetic problem-solving approaches, we're committed to delivering a solution that meets the unique needs of our users with compassion and efficiency.

SLD

SLD represents a pivotal project aimed at revolutionizing the workflow of our Client Success Managers, significantly reducing the time required for report preparation while enhancing overall efficiency. My role in this endeavor involves conducting comprehensive usability testing, refining user journeys through prototyping, and implementing front-end development solutions using Angular and Webpack. By collaborating closely with stakeholders and leveraging cutting-edge technologies, we've successfully delivered a solution that not only meets but exceeds the expectations of our users.

E-commerce

During my tenure at Superteia, I led the development of multiple e-commerce stores, each presenting unique challenges and learning opportunities. From crafting compelling user interfaces to prototyping and implementing front-end solutions using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on the TrayCorp platform, I played a pivotal role in bringing these digital storefronts to life. Through hands-on experience and a deep understanding of user needs, we successfully created engaging and seamless online shopping experiences for our clients.
